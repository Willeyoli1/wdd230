White Space

Taylormade Golf

Taylormade Golf does a really good job utilizing white space as a way of drawing your eyes to the images. They also use it to enhance the small amount of color they have on the page to make it pop even more.

PARC: Contrast

Tesla

For this example, Tesla does well showcasing Contrast. They do this by having soft colors through out the page, then changing to a sharp red color to draw the eyes of the viewer directly to the car.

PARC: Alignment

Thrasher Magazine

Thrasher Magazine's Website is a great example of Alignment because it keeps all the words left aligned. This makes it very easy to read and lets the viewer know where to keep their eyes while scrolling through their website.
